How we make them

Cast by hand.
Built for keeps.

Every Foundry Art tile begins as a hand-carved original. From that master, we create a bronze casting — solid, not plated — finished by hand and checked before it leaves our studio in Chicago.

1
Hand CarvedEach design originates as a hand-carved master by our studio artisans.
2
Cast in Solid BronzeMolten bronze is cast from the master — solid through, not a surface treatment.
3
Hand Finished & InspectedEach piece is finished by hand, checked, and packed before it ships to you.
